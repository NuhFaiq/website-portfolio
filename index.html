About me

Hello, I’m Faiq, a student pursuing a Diploma in Cybersecurity & Digital Forensics at Temasek Polytechnic. My passion for ethical hacking and digital forensics was sparked by childhood movies about hackers.

I have strong skills in applied ethical hacking, participating in Capture the Flag challenges and vulnerability identification using Kali Linux. I’m also experienced in Linux server administration, virtual environments with Oracle VM VirtualBox, and Windows IT system security. My expertise in digital forensics includes data cloning and using tools like Autopsy and FTK Imager for image recovery.

I’m proficient in incident response management and have worked with Snort and Wireshark to detect malicious activities. My soft skills include effective communication, conflict resolution, and fostering collaborative environments.
